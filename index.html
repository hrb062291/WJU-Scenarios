🚨 Critical Context: Jessup Achieved Full D2 Membership July 2025
After a 6-year transition process, Jessup was granted full NCAA D2 membership in July 2025. Withdrawal now triggers: (1) Bylaw 3.3.3.5 — must complete full re-entry process to rejoin D2 (3+ years); (2) PacWest conference exit obligations including likely exit fees and 12–18 months notice; (3) Athletic scholarship tail costs — current athletes on aid cannot be cut for program elimination; they must be honored through graduation or voluntary transfer. Full savings cannot be realized until FY28–29 at earliest.

⚠ Jessup-Specific LTV Considerations
With athletes at 23.9% of enrollment: eliminating sports eliminates ~280 students (est. 80% wouldn't re-enroll without their sport), reducing annual net tuition revenue by ~$3.6M. This must be offset against the athletic operational savings of ~$1.6M to get a true net figure. The real question is whether replacement non-athlete enrollment can fill that gap — and at what recruitment cost.
